A FESTIVAL FOR STUDENTS!


As the weather got warmer, we organized a paint festival for our students who were overwhelmed by the lessons. We also held a DJ concert at this festival, which is possible for every student to participate in.

THE CODING COMPETITION HAS COME TO AN END!


We have come to the end of the Decode writing competition organized between our students. We would like to express our thanks to the students who participated in this beautiful event. It turned out to be quite a useful event.

Our new library, the construction of which has been completed in our school, has been opened to the public. There are books in it that can help with all kinds of issues. The interest of students to our library, which you can use completely free of charge, is great. Opening: 08.00 Closing: 03.30
